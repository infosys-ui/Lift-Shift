Questions on T-Mobile service?

  • How do I check my balance, data, or minutes?

    You can check your balance, data, minutes, and text messages for free. Find out how on our Check your usage page.

  • What are the different ways I can pay my bill?

    T-Mobile provides several options for paying your bill, including EasyPay, making an online payment, paying over the phone, and more.

  • Is 4G LTE available yet?

    We plan to roll out our state-of-the-art 4G LTE network nationwide by the end of 2013. Not sure if it is available in your area yet? Check out our 4G LTE Coverage list to find out.

  • Can I use my iPhone on T-Mobile's network?

    As over one million customers have already learned, it’s quick and easy to setup your iPhone® to work on T-Mobile’s network.

  • What should I do if my phone is lost or stolen?

    The theft or loss of a device is a difficult experience. Fortunately, T-Mobile provides a few easy-to-setup security features that can protect your information and simple steps you can take to secure your information and account if your T-Mobile device is ever lost or stolen.

  • How do I upgrade my phone?

    Upgrading your phone through your My T-Mobile account is simple and fast. See our phone upgrade FAQs for additional information.

  • Where's my phone order?

    It is really easy to track your phone order. Visit our Order status & tracking page for more information.

  • How do I get phone insurance?

    Premium Handset Protection (PHP) Phone insurance provides protection against loss, theft, accidental damage, and malfunction of your wireless phone. Learn more about T-Mobile's phone insurance PHP program here.
