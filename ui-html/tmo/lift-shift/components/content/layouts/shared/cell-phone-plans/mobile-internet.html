Questions on plans?

  • What happens if I go over the limit of my high-speed data?

    At T-Mobile we don't charge overage fees for domestic use. With our Simple Choice Plan, once you have used all the 4G LTE data included in your plan, your data speed will automatically convert to up to 2G web speeds for the remainder of your billing cycle. At the beginning of your next billing cycle you will start over with a new allotment of 4G LTE data. In contrast, our new Simple Starter plan includes up to 500MB of 4G LTE data. Once a user reaches the 500MB of high-speed data, the service is suspended, with the peace of mind that no overage fees will apply. For additional data, on-demand data passes are available via My.T-Mobile.com, visiting a TMO store or calling Care.

  • What if I want to change my plan?

    It’s our mission to not be like all the other wireless carriers and one way were doing that is by not locking you into a restrictive 2-year service contract with our plans. What that means is that it’s easier for you to change plans when you need to.

    To change your plan or get more information log onto My T‐Mobile
  • No service contract? Really?

    It’s our mission to not be like all the other wireless carriers and one way we're doing that is by not locking you into a restrictive 2-year contract with our plans. And since there are no annual service contract requirements, there are no early termination fees and you can upgrade when you want.

  • How do I switch?

    We’ve made it simple for you to switch to T-Mobile. First you select a phone or device—you can either shop for a new one or bring your own. If you bring your own you should first check if it’s eligible. Then you choose how much high-speed data you want and head to our secure checkout. Then all you do is give us your old account information and we’ll contact your carrier to complete the process.

  • How does Music Freedom work?

    It couldn’t be easier! You don’t have to do anything to get the Music Freedom offer as a Simple Choice customer. Stream as much music as you want on your smartphone from top streaming services, including Pandora, iHeartRadio, iTunes Radio, Rhapsody, Slacker, Spotify and Milk Music - and it won’t count towards your 4G LTE data plan. Music Freedom is included in your Simple Choice rate plan at no extra charge.

  • What is 4G data?

    A 4G data network is the technology that provides the mobile internet access for your phone, tablet, laptop and other wireless devices. Our nationwide 4G LTE data network is fast so you can surf, download, stream and email quickly from any device, virtually anywhere.

  • How do I know if I'll have coverage in my area?

    T-Mobile covers 96% of Americans.

    That’s right, at T-Mobile we cover 96% of Americans coast to coast. Whether you’re making calls, texting, or mapping directions, we’ve got you covered where you live, work, and play. And you can connect in more places with WiFi Calling using virtually any available WiFi network. To find out what coverage is like near you, check our map.

    View coverage map
  • What is the new Simple Starter plan? How much does it cost and what is included?

    The Simple Starter plan is an entry-level price point for individual customers. For only $40 per month you can get a premium level, postpaid domestic service including unlimited talk, unlimited text and up to 500MB of 4G LTE data on America’s fastest nationwide 4G LTE network with no data overages.

  • Is Simple Starter different than Simple Choice? Why?

    Simple Starter is a separate offering than our Simple Choice Plan. Simple Starter is designed for the single line user that has been on the fence for data adoption, who will now find a low-risk, affordable option that allows them to take advantage of T-Mobile’s 4G LTE network without worrying about expensive data overage fees.

  • Why are you announcing this? Who is this plan for?

    At T-Mobile, we believe wireless should be worry-free and provide contract freedom, no matter how much or how little data you need. For customers new to smartphones who are unsure of how much data they may need, or those who use less data on average, it shouldn’t be a terrifying guessing game to use your device. That’s why T-Mobile is introducing our new postpaid Simple Starter plan for $40, which includes unlimited talk, unlimited text and up to 500MB of 4G LTE data on America’s fastest nationwide 4G LTE network.

  • Does this apply for families? What about single lines?

    The Simple Starter plan will be available for single line customers. Our Simple Choice Plan offers the better value for multi-line or family customers.

  • Is this plan available for business customers?

    Yes, the Simple Starter plan is available for small business customers. Small business customers may purchase it for single line use; no additional discounts are available.

  • Is Simple Global included (international roaming, stateside international talk and text, etc.) in the Simple Starter plan?

    The Simple Starter plan will not have access to international service packages. The plan is intended for domestic users as an entry-level option for wireless data usage. Our Simple Choice Plan offers the better value if you want to get international talk and text.

  • Does the Simple Starter plan require a contract? Does it also include EIP? What about JUMP!?

    At T-Mobile, we believe wireless plans should not be tied to a long-term service contract. Simple Starter is a no annual service contract plan, with many benefits that maximizes domestic use; including, access to EIP and the best upgrade program in wireless with JUMP!™

  • Does the Simple Starter plan support tethering?

    Yes, the Simple Starter plan supports tethering as part of the 500MB of data allotment.
