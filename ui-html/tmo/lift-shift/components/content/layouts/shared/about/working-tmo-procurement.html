Procurement at T-Mobile

At T-Mobile, we rely on thousands of suppliers to provide the goods and services required to support our business operations and serve our growing customer base. Because the goods and services provided by our suppliers affect our own products and services, we maintain high standards, seeking the highest quality solutions and service at the best value.

T-Mobile is committed to its supplier relationships and we actively manage and monitor ongoing performance. We are always looking to expand our supplier base. If you are interested in working with T-Mobile, follow the registration process outlined below.

Supplier Diversity Program

T-Mobile is committed to building relationships with small and diverse businesses that provide quality and competitively priced products and services. If your business is a certified minority-owned, woman-owned, service-disabled veteran-owned, veteran-owned, small, disadvantaged, or HUBZone business and you would like to work with T-Mobile, we would like to hear from you.

Self-Certify for T-Mobile

If your company is interested in working with T-Mobile, the first step is to complete the self-certification process. Once your company information is added to the Duns & Bradstreet database and T-Mobile has identified a need for the goods or services provided by your company, a T-Mobile representative will reach out to you to explore potential opportunities. If there is not an immediate need, your company information will remain in the database.

Note that the supplier database is hosted by a third party, Duns & Bradstreet. Entering your company information into the database will allow your company information to be searchable by all Duns & Bradstreet customers. We also request you to register with the California Public Utilities Commission (CPUC) to receive their CPUC certification.
