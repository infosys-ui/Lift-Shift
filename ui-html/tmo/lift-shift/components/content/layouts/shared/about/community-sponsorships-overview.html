Proposals & Requests

Thank you for your interest in and consideration of T-Mobile for your sponsorship opportunity. All proposals for sponsorship must be submitted exclusively via the T-Mobile Sponsorship Proposal Submission email here. Your proposal will reach the appropriate personnel for review and consideration.

You can normally expect a response within two to three weeks. Phone, fax, or postal-mailed proposals will not be considered at this time.
