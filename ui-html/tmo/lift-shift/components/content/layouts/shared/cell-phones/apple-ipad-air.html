Questions on shopping?

  • What is mobile internet?

    Mobile internet is the wireless data service that allows you to connect to the full Internet on a mobile internet-capable device through T-Mobile's network. Mobile internet-capable Internet devices from T-Mobile® include tablets, Ultrabooks, mobile hotspots, and laptop sticks.

  • How is mobile internet different from Wi-Fi?

    Mobile internet can be more convenient than Wi-Fi because it allows you to connect to the Internet anywhere you get a T-Mobile data network signal, instead of having to hunt for a free Wi-Fi location. When traveling to areas with T-Mobile coverage, this saves you the chore of purchasing access and memorizing multiple Wi-Fi logins.

  • How can I save $10 on my mobile internet rate plan?

    Add a mobile internet package (Internet device and plan) to any voice package (individual or family), and you immediately qualify for the $10 mobile internet rate plan monthly discount.

  • When will I see the $10 discount applied?

    The $10 discount will be applied as a bill credit to your monthly bill.
