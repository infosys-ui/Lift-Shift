iPhone activation tips: What you'll need

Your phone

Have your iPhone handy and turned on. The SIM is already installed.

Activation code

The activation code is located in the activation booklet included with your phone.

ICCID number

Find the ICCID number on the back of the iPhone box. During the online set up, enter the ICCID number into the SIM card field when it is displayed.

iPhone number (IMEI)

You will also need the iPhone IMEI number to complete the setup process. It can be found on the back of the box or on your iPhone by tapping on Settings> General> About, and scrolling down to the IMEI section.

If you don't have the original box, you can get the IMEI number by dialing *#06# on the phone you want to activate.

Your current phone number

You'll need to know the phone number that you wish to transfer from another carrier or from an existing T-Mobile line to your new pay in advance plan.

Activation tips for Android, Windows, BlackBerry, and all basic phones: What you'll need

Your phone

Insert the mini SIM card and battery into the phone. Charge the phone so it's ready to use once activated.

Activation code

The activation code is located in the activation booklet included with your phone.

SIM card number

The SIM number is located on the SIM card.

phone serial number(IMEI)

Locate the phone serial (IMEI) number on the box your phone came in. It's also inside the phone, under the battery.

If you don't have the original box, you can get the IMEI number by dialing *#06# on the phone you want to activate.
