Questions on Premium Handset Protection

    Customers enrolled after 7/14/2013 may file a claim online or call 1-866-866-6285.

    Customers enrolled before 7/14/2013 may file a claim online or call 1-866-268-7221.

    You will need the following information:

    • Make/model of the damaged, lost or stolen equipment

Premium Handset Protection Coverage Summary: This is a brief description of the Coverages and Exclusions that apply to Premium Handset Protection. A copy of the coverage documents with complete details regarding benefits, exclusions and deductibles will be provided to you when you enroll in the program.

  • T-Mobile's employees are not fully-licensed insurance agents. Premium Handset Protection may duplicate other insurance coverages, such as homeowner's or renter's insurance, that you may have.   • You are eligible for a maximum of 2 approved claims within a 12-month period for accidental damage, loss or theft. A per occurrence limit of $1,500 applies to each covered loss. There is no limit for approved claims for mechanical and electrical breakdown.
  • To file a claim, simply visit myPHPinfo.com, 24 hours a day, 7 days a week. You may also call 1-866-866-6285 and a Customer Service Representative will work with you to process your claim. The claim must be filed by you within 90 days of the incident, unless otherwise required by state law. Following information must be handed: Device's mobile number, manufacturer, make and model; Electronic Serial Number or IMEI; credit card/debit card/eCheck for deductible payment.

  • A non-refundable deductible of $20, $50, $100, $150 or $175, depending on your device, apply to each approved accidental damage, loss and theft claim. Deductible may be collected from you by credit card, debit card or eCheck. A $5 processing fee will apply for each approved mechanical or electrical breakdown claims. Premium Handset Protection covers the device and certain accessories, when required. Accessories covered as part of a loss include standard chargers, batteries, and SIM cards.
  • Please remember that damaged or malfunctioning devices must be returned within ten days of receiving your replacement device. If the device is not returned, you will be charged an unrecovered equipment fee not to exceed $900.
  • Causes of Loss Not Covered: Losses caused or resulting from the following are not covered: corrosion or rust; pre-existing conditions; failure to follow manufacturer's maintenance recommendations; intentional or dishonest acts; repair or service; power fluctuations; delay or loss of market, loss of income or interruption of business; and inherent design defects. Additional Cause of Loss Not Covered for Pay-in-Advance customers: costs recoverable under any product warranty. Refer to coverage documents for a complete list of exclusions.
